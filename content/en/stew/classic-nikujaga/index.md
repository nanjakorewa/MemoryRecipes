{{< kitchen-notes title="Cook's Notes" >}}
- Keep a gentle simmer once the drop lid is on; vigorous boiling will break the potatoes.
- Resting time is part of the cook—plan it so you can prep sides while the stew calms.
- Leftovers thicken in the fridge; thin with a splash of dashi when reheating.
